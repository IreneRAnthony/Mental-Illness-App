Breathing

How to best handle panic attacks.(For later)

  • Recognize what you are feeling.
  • Think of a mantra to repeat in your head. (Ex. this too shall pass, I'm going to be okay, etc.)
  • Find a focus object to stare at, or close your eyes.
  • Continue to take deep and steady breaths until your breathing is back under control, and your heart is no longer racing.
  • If possible, find somewhere to calm down and take a small break until you are completely alright.
